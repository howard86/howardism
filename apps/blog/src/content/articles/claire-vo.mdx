## Summary

Host of the **"How I AI"** interview series, published on ChatPRD (chatprd.ai), in which practitioners walk through their concrete AI workflows. She interviewed [Thariq Shihipar](/articles/thariq-shihipar) for the episode that produced the [HTML as the New Markdown](/articles/html-as-the-new-markdown) and [Compute Allocator](/articles/compute-allocator) material in this wiki. Personal site clairevo.com; on X as @clairevo.

She is not a passive interviewer here — she contributes a parallel practice of her own, which is why she earns an entity page rather than a byline.

## Her own practice (per the interview)

Claire reports building a **component-visualization page** similar to Thariq's [Living Design System](/articles/living-design-system): a single page showing all of an app's components in their various states. Her stated motivation is organizational rather than technical — it lets **marketers and other non-technical stakeholders** who need high-fidelity mockups for decks or videos "go to a single page… and get exactly what they need without bugging an engineer." This is the same engineering↔rest-of-company bridge that the [Living Design System](/articles/living-design-system) and [HTML as the New Markdown](/articles/html-as-the-new-markdown) workflows claim as their payoff, reported independently by the interviewer.
